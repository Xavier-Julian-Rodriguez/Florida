Florida

Admitted as the 27th state on March 3rd, 1845.

image source: WikiImages (January 03, 2013). Pixabay. https://pixabay.com/photos/rocket-launch-rocket-take-off-67643/

14,000 year ago, Paleo-Indians entered Florida. The earliest historical record for major groups of people residing in Florida was in the 16th century. These people included the Timucua in the northern central region, the Calusa residing in the southwest region, the Ais in the central Atlantic coast, and the Apalachee habitating the Florida Panhandle.
